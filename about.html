ENABLE-INDIA AND IT'S FOUNDERS

SANJAY ARORA
Sanjay holds a Bachelors of Technology (Honours) in Electronic Engineering from NIT Kurukshetra and an MBA in Finance with distinction from Punjab University.
He started his career with government in National Informatics Center (NIC) where his last assignment was Director of Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh region. He was instrumental in making these states go paperless with modernization of utility building, driving licenses and other governmental processes. Before giving up his corporate career his last role was a CEO of in a German Multinational Company Groz Beckert where he was previously a CIO and Vice President Production, Projects and Logistics.
He chose to move out from corporate life to make a difference in lives of students who have potential to achieve their dreams but need right direction. His other plans for next innings include helping elderly in a way to enrich their lives.
He is an avid reader of history and enjoys in-depth political analysis of national and international political situations. He is proficient in German and is currently based in Chandigarh
NAQI ABBAS
Equipped with a Degree in Electronics Engg. from Aligarh Muslim University, Naqi started his career as a Scientist in Defence Research & Development Organization (DRDO).
He later did his Post-Graduate Diploma in Personnel Management & Industrial Relations (PGDPMIR) from XLRI Jamshedpur.
He has worked in various profiles at Dr.Reddy’s Labs and at CEAT Ltd. Some of his key contributions include implementation of Self Managed Teams, Introduction of HR best practices at CEAT Sri Lanka, Setting up HR processes for Halol Greenfield Radial Tyre plant, Design & Delivery of New Managers Program, e-learning etc, Setting up the HR processes for Greenfield Project at CEAT Bangladesh etc. In June 2017, he quit his lucrative corporate career in order to focus full-time on mentoring the upcoming graduates. His last corporate assignment was as HR Head of one the API manufacturing plants in Dr Reddy’s Labs.
Naqi has been helping young graduates for past many years by guiding them for internship assignments. He has also been a speaker at industry forums. Bridging the gap between employment & employability is one of his key concerns.
In his spare time, Naqi likes to get closer to nature through gardening and trekking.
He is currently based at Hyderabad.
